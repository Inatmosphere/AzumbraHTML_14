Program objectives for the Technology Innovation graduate degree are derived from the other UAT graduate degree modules. Objectives are determined based on the coursework that graduate students complete within their selected modules. Students in this program will identify three (3) of the published objectives associated with UAT graduate program modules that they have completed and are assessed on their attainment of these objectives.
